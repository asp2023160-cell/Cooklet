Our Journey

Cooklet began as a tribute to the rich flavors and vibrant spices of Sri Lanka. Tired of losing family recipes passed down through generations, we envisioned a digital space that preserves the soul of Sri Lankan cooking while making it accessible to modern food lovers.

From the fiery heat of a black pork curry to the sweet comfort of watalappam, Cooklet is your interactive companion for exploring the Pearl of the Indian Ocean through its taste.
